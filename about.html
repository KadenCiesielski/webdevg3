About Us

A family tradition of quality, service, and great food — since 1968.



Our Story


D’Agostino’s Delicatessen is a family-owned and operated establishment run by Paul Sr., Lisa, and Nicholas D’Agostino. Our story began in 1968 when Samuel R. D’Agostino opened the original deli in Winchester, Massachusetts. Over the years, the D’Agostino family expanded to Arlington Heights (1981) and East Arlington (1987), serving generations of loyal customers.

After decades of dedication, Paul Sr. and his family proudly opened D’Agostino’s of Andover on January 30, 2015. Since then, we’ve been honored to bring our family recipes and friendly service to this wonderful community.

We thank you for your support and welcome your ideas for new products and flavors.
